Mario


The Mushroom kingdom had a beautiful princess named Toadstool. One day, the kingdom was invaded by the Koopa kingdom and the princess was kidnapped. She was safely kept under the custody of the king Koopa himself. Now, the king of the mushroom kingdom declared that anyone who saves his daughter can marry her and become the prince of the mushroom kingdom. Apprised of this, Mario, the only prince of the maria kingdom is all set to rescue her but was unaware of the way to reach her. He is seeking your help to reach the King of the Koopa so as to rescue the princess.

Input


Input will be given as a matrix of size m x n, 2 ≤ m ≤ 10, 2 ≤ n ≤ 10. The location of Mario is defined by M and T defines the location of princess Toadstool. o is an obstacle and Mario cannot pass through it. He can only pass through a path represented by x.

Output


Print a single word output Yes if there is a possibility for Mario to rescue her if there exists a path between them. Print No if no path exists between them.


Sample Input 1 Sample Output 1 
oxxMo
oxoox
xxooo
Toooo
 
Yes
Sample Input 2 Sample Output 2 
oooo
oooM
oooo
Tooo
 
No
Sample Input 3 Sample Output 3 
oooo
ooxM
oxxo
Tooo
 
No

Note

Mario can move either horizontally or vertically but cannot take cross paths.
