Spot the Islands


Isha is a five-year-old girl who is on her first visit to Maldives with family. She is offered a helicopter ride from where a nice view of all islands is covered. Being an enthusiastic child, she wished to count the islands and list them in the decreasing order on the basis of their lengths. Can you please help the little girl in spotting the islands?

Input


The first line of the input consists of two integers m , 3 ≤ m ≤ 10 and n , 3 ≤ n ≤ 10. It is followed by a matrix of order m x n. The contents of the matrix can be either x or o. 'x' represents water whereas 'o' represents land.

Output


The Output should contain the number of islands followed by the lengths of the islands from the biggest one to the smallest. If there is no island in the Ocean, then only 0 shall be printed.
