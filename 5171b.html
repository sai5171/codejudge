Catch the Train


Julie and Rom are love birds. One day, Julie is supposed to leave the city for a purpose. Rom promised Julie that he will be attending the station for giving her a send-off, but eventually, forgets it. He now realises that there were only X minutes left for the train to leave the station and starts rushing towards it.

At what speed should he travel so that he can just manage to meet Julie if the distance between Rom and the station is d kilometers.

Input


Input consists of two integers X , 60 ≤ x ≤ 6000000 and d , 0 ≤ d ≤ 100000.

Output


The Output must be a single integer representing the speed that Rom has to travel to meet Julie(in km/hr).


Sample Input 1 Sample Output 1 
60 5
 
5
Sample Input 2 Sample Output 2 
60 1
 
1
Sample Input 3 Sample Output 3 
120 4
 
2
