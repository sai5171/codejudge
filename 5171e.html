Find the Unique Count


Raju is a school lad who was given a task by his teacher. His task is to find the unique count of characters in a string. For instance, the unique characters of the string "arena" are 'a', 'r', 'e', 'n' and the unique count is 4.
His assignment has to be submitted by the evening and he is seeking your help to do it.

Input


Input consists of multiple lines.
The first line is an integer t , 1 ≤ t ≤ 100 indicating the number of test cases, followed by t strings of length utmost 100.

Output


The output should contain t integers with the unique counts of the corresponding input strings.


Sample Input 1 Sample Output 1 
1
csearena
 
6
Sample Input 2 Sample Output 2 
4
saikiran
veda
lokesh
nikhil
 
6
4
6
5

Note

Note: Only lower case characters are used in the input strings.
